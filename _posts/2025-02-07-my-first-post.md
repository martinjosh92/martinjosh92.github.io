## What is this?

I'm writing this blog to share my journey as a data nerd. I'm still early 
in this journey but I've heard (and believe) that in order to learn right, 
you need to learn _in public_ not just sit in a quiet room by yourself 
reading SQL textbooks and practicing python completely in the privacy of 
VSCode and a mess of project folders.  

Putting your work out there is how you get _real_ feedback and that feedback 
is how you do _real learning_. That's what I hope to achieve by writing: I 
can share what I learn and learn more by doing so.

<!--more-->

## Data is rad

I have always wanted to do _something_ with data and/or ML and/or AI, and 
I'm finally making a serious push to learn everything I can. I'll be finding 
data projects I find interesting and working on them, updating this blog with 
what I learn, and hopefully find a way to break into this exciting field.
